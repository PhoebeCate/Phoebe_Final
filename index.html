About Me

Hello! I am a 20-year-old dancer and singer with a deep love for expressing myself through movement and music. Dancing has always been my passion, allowing me to communicate emotions and stories in a way words cannot. Singing complements this by giving voice to my feelings and enhancing my creative journey.

When I’m not performing, you’ll often find me on the volleyball court, where I enjoy the thrill of teamwork and competition. Playing volleyball keeps me energized and sharp, both mentally and physically. It's my way of balancing my active lifestyle with the joys of community and collaboration.

In my downtime, I unwind by diving into the world of K-dramas. The captivating storylines and rich characters inspire me and fuel my creativity. These moments of relaxation recharge me, allowing me to pursue my passions with even more enthusiasm. Let’s connect and explore the beauty of creativity together!
